🎯 Objectives
Analyze patient wait times and satisfaction levels
Identify peak busy hours and days
Track admission vs non-admission trends
Understand department referral patterns
Explore patient demographics (age, gender, race)
Build an interactive dashboard for decision-making

🛠️ Tools & Technologies
SQL → Data cleaning, transformation, querying
Power BI → Data visualization & dashboard creation
Excel/CSV → Data source

📊 Dashboard Features
🔹 1. Monthly View
Monthly trends of patients
Avg wait time & satisfaction score
Admission status breakdown
Day & hour-wise patient distribution
🔹 2. Consolidated View
Total patients, avg wait time, satisfaction score
Patient referrals overview
Age group, gender, and race distribution
Heatmap for peak hours
🔹 3. Patient Details
Detailed patient-level data
Filters for deeper analysis
🔹 4. Key Takeaways
Summary insights from the dataset
Highlights operational bottlenecks
Identifies improvement areas

📈 Key Insights
Average wait time is around 35 minutes, indicating scope for optimization
Nearly 50% of patients are admitted, showing high ER utilization
Mondays and weekends are the busiest days
Peak hours observed around 11 AM, 1 PM, 7 PM, and late night
Majority of cases do not require referrals, but General Practice leads among those referred
